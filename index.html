Practice

I work with adults who feel stuck, overwhelmed, or caught in patterns that repeat in relationships, work or in how they experience themselves. This might show up as anxiety, low mood, difficulties in relationships, or a sense that things are not shifting despite effort.

The work is slower and more considered and often focuses on what is difficult to say, avoid, or keep repeating.

I offer longer term psychoanalytic psychotherapy for people who want to understand what is going on beneath that.

Background

I am a psychoanalyst in training at the Site for Contemporary Psychoanalysis.
